The Rules

Objective: figure out the 4 circle colour code that the computer has set in 10 rounds or fewer.

Controls: there are 6 colour buttons to choose your colours for each guess, a button to lock in your guess, a button to change the colour of the last circle you set and a to reset the whole row of circles to choose all new colours for them.

How to play: use the controls to enter your guess for this round and submit it. You will receive feedback on your guess on the right hand side of the row in the feedback section. A colour may appear more than once in the 4 circle colour code that the computer has set.

Feedback:

  • Red circle - a correct colour in the correct location
  • White circle - a correct colour NOT in the correct location
  • Grey circle - an incorrect colour

You will get feedback for each of your circles but the order of the feedback circles does not indicate which circle the feedback relates to.

How to win: crack the code before the ten rounds are up!
